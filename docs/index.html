Core Principles

Decentralized P2P

No central servers, peer-to-peer architecture

280,000+ IPFS nodes strengthen the network. Each RustDHT peer adds capacity and resilience. Direct connections eliminate intermediaries and single points of failure.

Data Sovereignty

You own your data, cryptographically

Cryptographic ownership means no platform can lock or delete your content. Your private key is your identity and authority over your data space.

Offline-First

Work without connectivity, sync when available

CRDTs enable seamless offline editing with automatic conflict resolution. Continue working during network outages, sync automatically when reconnected.

High Performance

Rust + WebAssembly for near-native speed

Sub-100ms latency, minimal footprint, runs in browser or natively. Memory safety without garbage collection enables predictable performance.

About RustDHT

A decentralized P2P graph database inspired by GunDB, built with Rust for performance and WebAssembly for universal compatibility. Combines proven patterns from IPFS, Holochain, and modern CRDT research.
